# Pricing Strategy

Substrate uses **value-based pricing** aligned with customer outcomes and expansion potential.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

### Core Principles

1. **Align with Value Delivered**
   - CodeGraft: Per-repo (complexity proxy)
   - Chronicle: Per-user (engagement proxy)
   - Enterprise: Per-asset/transaction (scale proxy)

2. **Start Low, Expand Over Time**
   - Free tier creates demand
   - Low entry price reduces friction
   - Natural expansion triggers built-in

3. **Transparent and Predictable**
   - No hidden fees
   - Clear usage limits
   - Monthly or annual billing

4. **Expansion Built-In**
   - Easy to start small
   - Natural growth paths
   - Volume discounts at scale

## Pricing Evolution

### Year 1-2: Land

- Goal: Adoption > margin
- CodeGraft Pro: $499/month (below SonarQube)
- Willing to lose money on small customers
- Focus on product-market fit

### Year 3-4: Expand

- Raise prices as value proven
- CodeGraft Pro: $799/month (+60%)
- Justify with: features, accuracy, case studies
- Grandfather early customers

### Year 5+: Premium

- "The structural integrity platform" commands premium
- Enterprise deals: $500K-2M annually
- Competitive with Datadog, Wiz, LeanIX

## Billing

### Payment Methods
- Credit card (monthly/annual)
- ACH/wire (annual only)
- Invoice (Enterprise, net 30)

### Cancellation
- Monthly: Cancel anytime, prorated refund
- Annual: Cancel anytime, no refund
- Data export provided within 30 days

### Overages
- Nodes: Billed monthly in arrears
- Users: Prompted to upgrade when exceeded
- Grace period: 7 days
